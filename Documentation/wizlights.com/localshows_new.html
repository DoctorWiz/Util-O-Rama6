Westside:

Christmas on Crystal Springs
10580 Crystal Springs Road, Jacksonville Off of Chaffee Rd. Near I-10
This is probably the biggest and best synchronized light show in the Northeast Florida area.  Winner of ABC's Great Christmas Light Fight in 2014.  Over 100,000 lights.  M.C.ed by a family of robotic snowmen.  This show has been running for many years and gets bigger and better every year.  It is well known and gets lots of visitors and thus, lots of traffic.  Your best bet is to park in the empty lot in front of the retention pond just west of the display.  Then walk down the sidewalk a short ways and you can stand and watch the full show.
Hours: 7-10 PM Radio: 103.9 FM Website: ChristmasOnCrystalSprings.com Facebook page:

Doctor Wizard's Giant Musical Holiday Light Show (or just Wizlights for short)
1252 Tumbleweed Drive, Orange Park Take Blanding Blvd. to Tanglewood subdivision. Take the 3rd right (Tumbleweed) and follow it around.  Click here for map.
This is the biggest, best, and most unusual synchronized light show in Clay County.  Over 20,000 LEDs synchronized to music, plus lasers, strobes, disco balls, projectors and more!  A whole forest of decorated trees!  People of all faiths and beliefs are welcome.  The show is totally free but donations to Safe Animal Shelter of Middleburg are accepted.
Hours: 6-10 PM Radio: 103.9 FM Website: Wizlights.com Facebook page:
